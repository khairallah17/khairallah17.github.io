Statistics is a field that deals with collection, organization analysis interpretation and representation of the data -> Decision Making

## types 
  - Descriptive -> organizing and summarizing the data
    - measure of central tendency (mean, median, mode)
    - measure of dispersion (variance, standard deviation)
  - Inferential -> collect data (sample data/population data) to make conclusion or inferences using some experiments

## population and sample
  - population (N)
  - sample (n)

## Measure of central tendency
- Mean
- Median: picking the central element is the median -> purpose to handle outliers
  - sort numbers -> pick the central number
  - if the number of elements is even we take the two central element sum then divide them by 2
- Mode: get the element with maximum frequency
